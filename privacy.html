Privacy Policy

Effective date: 31 March 2026

Inspirasi (“the App”) is operated by Roland McCormack (“we”, “us”, or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use the Inspirasi mobile application and related services.

By downloading, accessing, or using Inspirasi, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. What Inspirasi does

Inspirasi is a freemium mobile application that helps users discover activity and outing ideas. Some features are available for free, while certain premium features require a paid subscription.

2. Information we collect

We may collect the following categories of information when you use the App.

A. App profile and identity information

Inspirasi currently allows use of the App without requiring account creation or sign-in. We may create and store an app profile linked to your use of the App on your device.

We may collect and store information associated with that app profile, including:

  • a unique user ID
  • profile metadata such as creation, update, and last active timestamps
  • subscription or entitlement status associated with your app profile

B. Usage and app activity information

We may collect information about how you use the App, including:

  • features used
  • actions taken in the App
  • suggestion generation events
  • upgrade or paywall interactions
  • usage limit or access restriction events
  • app performance and diagnostic information
  • crash and error reports

This information helps us operate the App, improve the user experience, troubleshoot issues, and understand how features are used.

C. Location information

Inspirasi may collect location information if you choose to use location-based features.

This may include:

  • your selected city or location label
  • precise location coordinates if you choose a “near me” or similar location feature and grant permission
  • place search or location lookup data entered by you

Location access is optional. You can refuse location permission and still use other parts of the App, including manually choosing a location where available.

D. Saved content and favourites

If you save favourites or similar content in the App, we may store information related to those saved items, such as:

  • suggestion title
  • suggestion description
  • map or place query information
  • associated location coordinates where relevant
  • timestamps relating to when the item was created, updated, or removed

E. Device, log, and technical information

We and our service providers may automatically collect technical information such as:

  • device type
  • operating system
  • app version
  • approximate diagnostic or network information
  • log data necessary to maintain service reliability and security

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • provide and operate Inspirasi
  • create and manage your in-app profile and related data
  • deliver suggestions and App functionality
  • provide location-based features when requested by you
  • save favourites and related App data
  • manage subscriptions, entitlements, and premium access
  • enforce fair-use, usage, or quota limits
  • improve App performance, features, and user experience
  • monitor service health, investigate problems, and prevent misuse
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our rights

4. Artificial intelligence features

Inspirasi uses artificial intelligence and related automated systems to generate suggestions and support parts of the user experience.

To provide these features, information you submit through the App, such as filters, selected preferences, and relevant location inputs, may be processed by our backend systems and service providers.

AI-generated content may not always be complete, accurate, suitable, or available for every situation. You should use your own judgment before acting on any suggestion provided by the App.

5. App continuity and device changes

Inspirasi currently does not require account creation or sign-in. As a result, your saved data and app continuity may be limited across devices, reinstalls, or device changes.

For example, favourites and similar app data may not carry over automatically if you reinstall the App, clear app data, or move to a new device.

6. Subscriptions and premium features

Inspirasi may offer paid subscriptions or premium features. Subscription purchases may be processed through third-party billing platforms such as Apple App Store or Google Play, and subscription management providers such as RevenueCat.

We may store and use information related to subscription status and entitlements in order to provide premium functionality, restore eligible purchases, prevent abuse, and support customer service.

7. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information with third-party service providers that help us operate the App, such as providers of:

  • cloud hosting and database infrastructure
  • analytics
  • crash reporting
  • subscription management
  • mapping, geolocation, and place lookup services
  • AI and backend processing

These providers may include services such as Firebase, Google, RevenueCat, OpenAI, Apple, and Google Play, depending on the feature being used.

We may also disclose information:

  • if required by law
  • to respond to lawful requests by regulators or authorities
  • to protect our rights, users, systems, or services
  • in connection with a business transfer, restructuring, or sale

8. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the App, provide features to you, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our agreements, and improve the service.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information, whether a subscription is involved, and whether the information is needed for security, diagnostics, or service continuity.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical, administrative, and organisational measures to protect information we hold. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children

Inspirasi is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

If local law in your jurisdiction requires a higher minimum age for digital consent, you should only use the App if you meet that age requirement or have appropriate consent.

11. Your choices

Depending on how you use the App, you may be able to:

  • decline location permissions
  • delete your app account and associated data through the in-app deletion flow
  • uninstall the App at any time
  • manage your subscription through the relevant app store account
  • contact us regarding privacy-related questions or requests

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including through the systems of our service providers. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws than your own.
