Inspirasi Account Deletion

Roland McCormack / Inspirasi

If you would like your Inspirasi account and associated app data deleted, please email:

Email: idealinc.inspirasi@gmail.com

Subject line: Account Deletion Request

How to request deletion

In your email, please include enough information for us to identify your account, such as:

  • the email address associated with your Google or Apple sign-in, if applicable
  • your device platform (Android or iPhone)
  • any other details that may help us locate your account

What will be deleted

When we process a valid deletion request, we will delete your Inspirasi account and associated app data, which may include:

  • your app account/profile record
  • saved favourites and associated app content
  • subscription entitlement records held for your app account, where applicable
  • other account-linked app data reasonably required to operate your account

What may be retained for a limited time

Some information may be retained for a limited period where reasonably necessary for:

  • legal, tax, accounting, or compliance obligations
  • fraud prevention, security, or abuse prevention
  • backup, restoration, or technical record-keeping processes
  • subscription, billing, or transaction records maintained by app store or payment providers

Some third-party platform records, such as Apple App Store or Google Play purchase history, may continue to be retained by those providers under their own policies.

Retention period

We aim to process deletion requests within a reasonable time. In some cases, certain records may be retained for a limited period after your deletion request where reasonably necessary for the purposes described above.

Partial deletion

Inspirasi does not currently offer a separate process for requesting deletion of specific individual data items without deleting the account.
