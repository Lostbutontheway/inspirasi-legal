Inspirasi Account and Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your Inspirasi account and associated app data, you can do this directly in the app.

In-app path:

Settings → Account Management → Delete Account & Data

Follow the prompts in the app to permanently delete your account data.

What will be deleted

When you confirm deletion, Inspirasi will permanently delete your account and associated app data, which may include:

  • your app account/profile record
  • saved favourites and associated app content
  • usage-related account data linked to your app account
  • other account-linked app data reasonably required to operate your account

Important subscription note

Deleting your account does not cancel your paid subscription.

If you have an active subscription, you must cancel it separately through your:

  • Apple App Store account settings
  • Google Play account settings

What may be retained for a limited time

Some information may be retained for a limited period where reasonably necessary for:

  • legal, tax, accounting, or compliance obligations
  • fraud prevention, security, or abuse prevention
  • backup, restoration, or technical record-keeping processes
  • subscription, billing, or transaction records maintained by app store or payment providers

Some third-party platform records, such as Apple App Store or Google Play purchase history, may continue to be retained by those providers under their own policies.

Retention period

We aim to process account deletion promptly. In some cases, certain records may be retained for a limited period after deletion where reasonably necessary for the purposes described above.

Partial deletion

Inspirasi does not currently offer a separate process for deleting specific individual data items without deleting the account.
